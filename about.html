About Us

Livingstone VTU App

At Livingstone VTU App, we offer a variety of benefits to our users:

  • Discounts: Get a ₦2 discount on every airtime purchase, saved to your cashback wallet.
  • Referral Commission: Earn through our three-tier referral program:
    • Starter Tier: Receive ₦20 commission per referral and a 10% coupon on airtime and data every weekend once you have 50 referrals.
    • Silver Tier: Receive ₦50 commission per referral and a 15% coupon on airtime and data every weekend once you have 100 referrals.
    • Diamond Tier: Receive ₦200 commission per referral and a 20% coupon on airtime and data every weekend once you have 200 referrals.
  • Flexible Usage: Use your commission and discounts to buy airtime, data, or subscribe to TV services. Withdraw your earnings anytime.
  • Emergency Data: Dial USSD code 156 to receive 10MB of data, deducted from your wallet. If your wallet is empty, we will lend you the data and deduct the amount once you fund your wallet.
  • One-Time Subscription: Pay a one-time subscription fee of ₦500 to access the dashboard.
  • Wallet Activation: Fund your wallet with a minimum of ₦500 to activate your account. An activation link will be sent to your email.
